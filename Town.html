RIMFOREST, CALIFORNIA

About the town and Picture:

That picture is of Lake Arrowhead which is about 10 minutes away, there were no good pictures of the town itself. Rimforest is SMALL like modern village small. We have a gas station, 3 small restraunts, a lumberyard/hardwarestore, the electric company branch building, the gas company branch building, and equipment rental, a post office (because they don't deliver door to door here), and a small collection of houses that run the full spectrum from really nice and impressive, to hovels. It's both impressive and depressing for a town with less than 400 people living there.

The tourist destination of choice is Lake Arrowhead itself, the entire town is essentially a resort centered around a mall. Click the link below for more info.
